About:
GarGeon aims to solve many of the waste industry problems in Malaysia by trying to convince other businesses that the technology produced will help them reduce costs of operation and save more time and energy.

With this idea, the founders concluded that there's a need of certain applications as the following:
No. Application Purpose
1 Landing Page
  • Customers can book a waste pickup for their businesses
  • Hauler can register to be a part of GarGeon ecosystem
2 Admin Portal
  • GarGeon admins can manage the customers and haulers
  • Assign haulers for waste pickups
  • Keep track of payments
3 Hauler Portal
  • Hauler can assign tasks to their drivers and track them
  • Hauler can manage their assets
  • Keep track of payments
4 Customer Portal
  • Customer can see the progress of the requested pickup
  • Customer can manage the services of different locations
  • Keep track of payments
5 Customer App
  • An extension of the customer portal so that customer can track their services anywhere
  • Customer can receive push-notifications of the pickups
  • Much more easier usage of the customer portal
6 Hauler Driver App
  • Driver can easily go on for duty with a tap of a button
  • Driver can go to customer chosen location easily
  • Driver can easily notify hauler and customer if there's any problem/emergency
Timeline
This project was initially handled by 4 people which consists of 2 founders which handles the business side and 2 developers. We manage to create the main features of 6 applications (mobile app and website) just within 6 months of development. The main framework that we used for the web application are Codeigniter as a PHP framework and Bootstrap for the UI. While the mobile version we used the Ionic Framework. After the base is finished, we focused on improving the applications and bug fixing.
